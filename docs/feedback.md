We want to hear from you. Every piece of feedback shapes what the next build looks
like.

## Bugs and feature requests → GitHub Issues

Open an issue at <https://github.com/lucy-chapar/LucyTTS/issues/new> and tag it:

- **`bug`** — something Lucy is doing wrong, crashing on, or refusing to do.
- **`feature request`** — something Lucy doesn't do today that you wish it did.

GitHub will ask you to sign in (or sign up — it's free). Issues are public, which is
what we want for product feedback: other testers can chime in, vote, and see what's
already been reported.

If you're not sure whether something is a bug or a feature request, pick whichever feels
closer and we'll relabel it. Don't let labeling block you from reporting.

## Security disclosures and private messages → email

Some things shouldn't be public:

- Security vulnerabilities (anything that could let someone steal a key, intercept
  audio, escalate privileges, etc.).
- Private feedback you'd rather not put on a public issue tracker.
- TestFlight invite requests.
- Press, legal, or business inquiries.

For any of these, email **`lucychapar@pm.me`**.

Please don't post security issues on GitHub Issues — that exposes them publicly before
they're fixed, which is the opposite of what we both want.

## What's helpful to include

You don't have to include all of these. The more you can include, the faster we can
help.

- **What you were trying to do** — "I was trying to speak a sentence in a meeting."
- **What happened instead** — "Lucy said 'Test failed' and the sentence didn't play."
- **What you expected** — "I expected the sentence to play in the voice I picked."
- **Your platform** — Mac model + macOS version, or iPhone model + iOS version.
- **Lucy's version** — visible in **Settings → About** inside the app.
- **A screenshot or short screen recording** if possible.
- **The text you typed**, if it's not private. (If it is private, just describe the
  general shape, like "a 200-character sentence with a question mark.")

## What's especially useful to hear about

- **Things that are confusing**, even if they "work." If you had to think for more than
  a second about a button, we want to know.
- **Things that feel rough**, even if they're not technically bugs. Latency, weird
  voice quality on specific phrases, awkward keyboard behavior.
- **Cost surprises.** If your Fish bill jumped in a way you didn't expect after a Lucy
  session, that's a Lucy problem we want to investigate (it usually means we should
  have warned you or guardrailed something).
- **Voice and phrase suggestions.** If you found a Fish voice you love, or a phrase you
  use 50 times a week that should be a built-in preset, send the details.

## What you won't get

- We don't have a support staff. Lucy is built by one person with AI assistance.
  Response times will vary, especially on evenings and weekends.
- We won't promise to ship every requested feature. We will read everything.
- We won't share private email contents with anyone else without your permission.
- Public GitHub issues are public — please don't paste anything sensitive into them.

Thank you for being a beta tester. You're the reason this gets better.
